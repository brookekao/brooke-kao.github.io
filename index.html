Making it better

I brought Lean and GV Design Sprint methodology to a non-tech-savvy nonprofit by through teaching-by-doing and nurturing stakeholder relationships over 8 months. The ongoing result? An increasingly simpler, more actionable product that helps financially underserved make decisions on their finances.
